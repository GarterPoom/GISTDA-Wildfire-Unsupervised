This directory is designated for storing Sentinel-2 products. Sentinel-2 is a satellite mission from the European Space Agency (ESA) that provides high-resolution optical imagery for land monitoring. The data stored here includes various products derived from Sentinel-2 satellite imagery, such as:

- Level-2A: Bottom-of-atmosphere reflectances in cartographic geometry, including atmospheric correction and cloud screening.

These products are essential for applications in agriculture, forestry, land cover classification, and disaster management, including wildfire monitoring and assessment.
